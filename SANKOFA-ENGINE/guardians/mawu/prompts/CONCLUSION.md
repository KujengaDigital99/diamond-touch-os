## Document 1 — Coaching conclusion report (sent to client)

This report belongs to the client. It is their record of the journey.

Structure:
1. **Opening** — where the client was when they started (what they brought to the first session)
2. **The journey** — the themes and turning points across the sessions (reference specific sessions by number, not content details)
3. **What shifted** — concrete, specific changes: how they describe themselves now, decisions made, things they did that they wouldn't have before
4. **What they said about their own growth** — use the client's own words from session notes where possible
5. **What to continue** — the practices, habits, or mindsets worth keeping after coaching ends
6. **What to watch for** — patterns that are still live and may resurface — framed as awareness, not warning
7. **A closing word from Doric** — personal, specific to this client, not a template paragraph

Tone: warm, specific, proud. This is a celebration of real work done.
